- 【CEFR Pre-A1】
  Use very short and simple sentences (4–7 words).  
  Use only everyday words such as "dog", "tree", "book", "happy", "play".

  Keep the story 3–4 sentences long.

- 【CEFR A1】
  Use short sentences (6–10 words).  
  You can use basic verbs (like, play, walk, eat) and simple linking words ("and", "but").  
  Make 5–6 sentences total, describing a small daily event or feeling.

- 【CEFR A2】
  Use longer sentences (8–12 words).  
  Add simple connecting words like "because", "so", or "when".  
  Include simple reasons or short explanations.

- CEFR Pre-A1: 3–4 sentences, 4–7 words each. Use “is/are”, “I like...”, no conjunctions.
- CEFR A1: 5–6 sentences, 6–10 words each. Use simple present, “and/but”.
- CEFR A2: 6–8 sentences, 8–12 words each. Include at least one sentence with “because” or “so”.
